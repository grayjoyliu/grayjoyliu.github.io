About

Vivian Maier was an American street photographer known for her candid and captivating photos of urban life. Explore her remarkable collection and journey through the lens of a master photographer.

Learn more

A riddle, wrapped in a mystery, inside an enigma.

Vivian Maier's work, though largely unrecognized during her lifetime, has since gained widespread acclaim for its unique perspective and insightful portrayal of everyday life.

  • Born: February 1, 1926
  • Death: April 21, 2009
  • City: New York, Chicago
  • Website: vivianmaier.com/
  • Occupation: Daycare
  • Degree: Master
  • Hobby: Collection
  • Genre: Photography

Vivian Maier's work reflects her keen eye for detail and her ability to capture the essence of city life in all its complexity. Her photographs offer a glimpse into the past while remaining relevant and evocative in the present day.

Explore Vivian Maier's world through her lens and experience the beauty of urban life as seen through the eyes of a true master.
